About

I chose this topic because I have always been interested in space exploration and the creation of space rockets. I wanted to create an interactive website where people like me could get information easily by clicking a button, such as time, distance traveled, and many more that apply. This interactive website allows the user to easily filter information and search for what they want.

What is SpaceX ?

SpaceX is an aerospace manufacturer founded by Elon Musk. Under its name, there are many different rockets that have been launched to space for commercial use, as satellites, and many more. One of the key points is that they are able to create reusable rockets, and the price for a ride is cheaper than that of other rocket manufacturers.
